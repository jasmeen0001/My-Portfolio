HELIO

The Patient Management System is a comprehensive web application designed to streamline the healthcare experience for patients, providers, and administrators. For patients, the platform offers essential features such as user sign-up and login, a personalized landing page displaying their details, the ability to book and manage appointments, access to health card information, and post-appointment details including prescriptions and diagnoses. Future enhancements will enable symptom-based doctor recommendations and display doctor ratings during the booking process. For healthcare providers, the application provides a dashboard for managing appointments, entering patient information during ongoing consultations, and utilizing advanced language models to generate diagnoses and prescriptions efficiently. Administrators will benefit from a metrics dashboard that tracks appointments and clinic performance through visual analytics. With plans for online appointment integration and additional features, this project leverages modern technologies, including a React front-end, MySQL database, and service-oriented architecture, ensuring a robust and scalable solution for enhancing patient care and operational efficiency in healthcare settings.
