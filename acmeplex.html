ACMEPLEX

The AcmePlex Movie Theater Ticket Reservation App is a user-centric platform designed to streamline the process of booking movie tickets for customers at various AcmePlex theaters. Catering to both ordinary users and registered users (RUs), the app allows users to search for movies, select theaters, view available showtimes, and choose seats through a graphical interface. Ordinary users can purchase tickets securely via credit or debit cards and have the option to cancel their tickets up to 72 hours before the showtime, subject to a 15% administration fee. Registered users benefit from waived cancellation fees, early access to movie news, and priority ticket reservations, with 10% of seats reserved exclusively for them prior to public announcements. The system also securely stores registered users' information, ensuring a seamless and efficient booking experience while enhancing customer loyalty and satisfaction for AcmePlex theaters.
