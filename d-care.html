D-CARE

This project involves a hospital management application that assists patients in finding doctors, scheduling appointments, and accessing treatments while streamlining administrative tasks for healthcare professionals. It focuses on Corporate Medicare Management, benefiting both staff and the public through Administration and Client modules. Effective utilization of stored data is crucial for enhancing diagnoses and treatments while minimizing costs. Data Mining (DM) is employed to analyze data from Hospital Information Systems (HIS), identifying patterns that improve patient care and optimize resource allocation. Although the Knowledge Discovery from Databases (KDD) process can be resource-intensive, it significantly enhances decision-making and overall healthcare delivery.
